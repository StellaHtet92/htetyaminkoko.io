About Me

Bridging Earth Observation & Climate Policy

I hold a Doctor of Engineering in Remote Sensing and GIS from the Asian Institute of Technology, Thailand. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the International Space Science Institute (ISSI) in Bern, Switzerland, where I investigate urban climate dynamics, Urban Heat Island intensity, and the role of blue-green infrastructure in climate adaptation.

My research bridges satellite-based Earth observation, deep learning, and spatial-temporal modeling to produce policy-relevant, reproducible science. I collaborate with the Urban Climate group at the University of Bern and the Wyss Academy for Nature.

Previously, I collaborated with the FAO and IIASA on crop suitability assessment and geospatial analysisacross Afghanistan and the Solomon Islands.
